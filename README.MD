# Trouble Bewing
## Characters
* Washerwoman?: You start knowing that a player is a Washerwoman [+0 to +1 Washerwoman]
* Librarian?: You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Outsider. (Or that zero are in play.) [-1 to +1 Outsiders]
* Investigator?: You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Minion. They may change characters when you die.
* Chef?: You start knowing how many pairs of good players there are.
* Empath?: Each night, you learn how many of your 2 alive neighbors are evil. Once per game, the storyteller might tell you the number 3 instead.
* Fortune Teller?: Each night, choose 2 players: you learn if either is a Demon. Once per game, there may be a good player that registers as a Demon to you.
* Undertaker?: Each night*, you learn a good and evil character, one of which died by execution today.
* Monk?: Each night*, choose a player (not yourself): if they would die tonight, you learn this and lose your ability.
* Slayer?: Once per game, during the day, publicly choose a player: they die.
* Soldier?: You are safe from the Demon. Other players will think you are the demon no matter how confirmed you are.
* Recluse?: You might register as evil & as a Minion or Demon, even if dead.
* Ravenkeeper?: If you die at night, a good player becomes the Ravenkeeper?.
* Virgin?: The 1st time you are nominated, if the nominator is a Townsfolk, you are both executed immediately.
* Butler: Each night, choose a player (not yourself): tomorrow, you may only vote if they are voting too. [The storyteller thinks you are cool]
* Drunk?: You do not know you are the Drunk. You think you are a Townsfolk character, but you are not. You might be evil and neighbor the demon [-1 Minion if you are evil]
* Mayor: If you die at night, another player might die instead.
* Saint?: If you die during the day, your team loses.
* Poisoner?: Each night, choose two players: one of them is poisoned tonight and tomorrow day, the other learns false information.
* Spy?: Each night, you see the Grimoire. You might register as good & as a Townsfolk or Outsider, even if dead. You may choose to open your eyes at night.
* Scarlet Woman?: If there are 5 or more players alive & the Demon dies during the day, you become the Demon. (Travellers don't count.)
* Baron?: There are extra Outsiders in play. [+1 to +2 Outsiders]
* Imp?: Each night, choose a player: the previously chosen player dies. If you kill yourself this way, a Minion becomes the Imp.
